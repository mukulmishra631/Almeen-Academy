Our Story

Almeen Academy is recognized as the best CBSE school in Bhathat, dedicated to providing world-class education and holistic development for students in the region. Established with a vision to transform education in Bhathat, our school stands out as the top choice for parents searching for the 'best school near me' or 'top school in Bhathat'.

Our modern campus, located on Bhathat Bailo Road, is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, digital classrooms, science and computer labs, and a vibrant learning environment. We follow the CBSE curriculum, ensuring our students receive a strong academic foundation while also excelling in sports, arts, and co-curricular activities.

At Almeen Academy, our experienced faculty is committed to nurturing every child's potential, focusing on values, discipline, and innovation. Our school is not just a place for academic excellence but a community where students grow into responsible, confident, and successful individuals.

If you are looking for the best school near Bhathat or Gorakhpur, Almeen Academy is your trusted partner in your child's educational journey. Join us and experience why we are consistently ranked among the top schools in the area.

School Policies

We follow a structured set of rules to maintain discipline and a healthy learning environment.

Rules & Regulations

School Uniform

Summer Uniform (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri)

  • Boys: Light grey lining shirt with monogram, dark grey pants, black shoes, white socks, school tie & belt.
  • Girls: Same shirt, dark grey tunic with monogram, black shoes, white socks, tie & belt, hair in 2 ponies with black ribbon.

Salwar Kameez Option (Girls)

  • Dark grey Koti with monogram
  • Light grey lining Kameez
  • Dark grey Salwar

House Uniform (Wed, Sat)

  • Boys: House T-shirt, Lower, white shoes & socks.
  • Girls: House T-shirt, Lower or white Salwar Suit with House Koti, white socks.

Winter Uniform

  • Dark grey sweater with monogram
  • Dark grey woollen cap
  • Navy blue blazer with monogram

School Timings

  • School starts at 7:30 AM sharp.
  • Classes end at 12:30 PM on all working days.

Attendance Policy

  • Minimum attendance required is 75%.
  • Absence without prior notice may lead to disciplinary action.
  • Medical leaves require proper documentation.

Discipline Rules

  • Students are to maintain a high standard of good manners and general behavior.
  • Students should speak only English in the School Premises.
  • Students must be punctual and attend class in neat, tidy proper school uniform.
  • Articles found in school premises or in the classroom must be handed over to the office of the school.
  • Indisciplined acts will be liable for disciplinary action up to striking off the name from school permanently.
  • Students should not write, cover or scratch on school walls or damage the school furniture.
  • All students must abide by the rules & regulations of the school.
  • Presence of students on 15th August and 26th January is compulsory and students must come in uniform on these national festivals.
  • Use of mobile phones inside campus is strictly prohibited.
  • Students must respect teachers and fellow classmates.
